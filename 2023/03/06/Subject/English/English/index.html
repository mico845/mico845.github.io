•China’s space industry（aerospace industry） was launched in 1956. Over the past decades, China’s space industry has created one miracle after another. In 1992 China began to carry out the manned spaceflight program. In 2003 China launched Shenzhou-5, a manned spaceship. The successful launch made China the third country to launch manned spaceships. Through many students are allergic to learning English and usually get lost, they still feel obliged to learn English well. So how can teacher make students’ English learning journey more enjoyable? Effective, precise, and beneficial communication depends upon grammar and a good vocabulary.
Therefore, for these students who have language deficit and need to learn from scratch, it make sense to teach the basic language framework, accurate grammar and appropriate words. Learning grammar and a good vocabulary is just like driving with a road map in a well-conditioned car.
We should often study the road map (check grammar) and tune up the car engine (adjust vocabulary). Equipped with two essential assets, students will become attracted to English, and have excellent control their English learning journey.

With its layers of history and overlapping of cultures, Europe is a treasure trove of everything from the medieval and ancient to the baroque and art nouveau. Couple this with Arctic conditions in the north and beautiful Mediterranean climates in the south. And there’s a whole side of nature to go with the main dish of culture that can be found in this wonderful continent.

NO. 20 Sagrada Familia

Officially the Basilica de la Sagrada Familia,
this famously opulent church in Barcelona
is the brainchild of the renowned Catalan architect Antoni Gaudi.
With work starting on church back in 1882,
137 years later, the Sagrada still remains unfinished.
It is expected that the building will be completed in 2026.
Built in the art nouveau and Gothic style,
the fantastical church has an impressive eight spires
and ten that are yet to be constructed.
This amazing building draws crowds of tourists
with twisting turrets, and surreal curves,
while intriguing gargoyles make for something
from another world and time entirely.
